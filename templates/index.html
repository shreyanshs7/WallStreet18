A VIRTUAL STOCK
MARKET GAME



INTRODUCTION


1

Wallstreet is a virtual game that will give the students an opportunity to see the transgressions of the market first hand. While creating a virtual stock market and providing them with virtual stocks, this game in turn allows students to learn to build up from the least and how to become a giant in the world of changing trends.

This will be a daily event with a daily winner. Person on the top of the share market with the largest number of shares in value goes home as the winner.
SCOPE OF WORK


2

The participants will be provided with certain initial amount of money which they will be using to buy virtual stocks. The stock market deatils for each virtual company will be generated and modified by the servers , details of which will be shown through a projector. Participants can sell the stocks they bought accordingly with the current prices of the stock. In this way a participant's profit value will change. At the end of the day the participant with maximum profit will be declared the winner.
NEED


3

Helps develop the awareness of the trends of the market. Develop the descision making skills in students. Develop resource management skills.
GOALS


4

To help students become aware of the market strategies. Develop self confidence among students among students while making big decisions. Start the habit of being aware of every development possibility among students.
